StarLongman Liberia

Star Longman Liberia is the nation's premier educational bookstore, dedicated to supplying high-quality textbooks and learning resources for Pre‑School, Primary, Junior High, Senior High, and professional levels. Since 2012, we have been the trusted partner for schools, educators, and students across Liberia.

Our mission goes beyond selling books — we empower the next generation through accessible, affordable, and curriculum‑aligned educational materials. With a warehouse in Crown Hill, Monrovia, we serve both urban and rural communities with speed and integrity.

300 Assorted Books Donated to National Public Library

300+ Books

CNDRA Partner

Star Longman Liberia Books Inc, a Ghanaian company operating in Liberia, demonstrated its commitment to educational advancement by donating three hundred assorted books valued over US$3,000 to the National Public Library on Ashman Street, Monrovia. The library offers free research services to the public and is managed by the Center for National Documents and Records Agency (CNDRA).

Presenting the books, Star Longman Liberia Books Inc Country Manager Rita Ganidekam Abraham said the donation is geared towards improving Liberia’s education system.
— Rita Ganidekam Abraham, Country Manager

“We mainly distribute free Junior and Senior high text books to institutions as part of our corporate social responsibility.” This initiative reflects our ongoing dedication to empowering Liberian students.
